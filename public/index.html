Areoy — Helping Kids Learn, Explore, and Grow Through Clever

Areoy is a student-friendly learning platform that gives kids quick access to educational tools, school resources, classroom websites, and interactive learning experiences through Clever integration.

Educational Learning Tools

Students can access math practice, reading activities, science resources, writing tools, spelling games, coding lessons, research websites, and educational apps all in one place.

Subjects for Every Student

Areoy supports mathematics, language arts, science, history, geography, coding, engineering, art, music, and digital literacy.

Interactive Learning

Students can explore quizzes, flashcards, videos, educational games, practice tests, and classroom activities.

Reading and Writing Support

Reading comprehension tools, vocabulary builders, grammar practice, and writing support resources.

Math and STEM Education

Algebra, geometry, coding projects, science simulations, robotics activities, and STEM learning tools.

Safe Student Experience

Clean, simple, distraction-free interface designed for students in classrooms and at home.

Learning Through Clever

Quick access to school apps, assignments, and learning tools through Clever integration.
